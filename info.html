Brand Overview & Evolution

Love Velo is a cycling business dedicated to experiences that help riders push their boundaries ‚Äì in sport and life. We're not just obsessed with clocking more kilometres or tackling tougher climbs, we're all about broadening horizons and pushing the boundaries of what is possible. It's about gaining new experiences, and growing as individuals. Using cycling as a means to explore and engage with the world around you, expanding both your physical and mental boundaries.

Primary Brand Tagline

"We Ride Further. Together."

This represents an evolution from the original tactical message "Travel. By Bike" to a more community-focused positioning that maintains the core "Ride Further" philosophy while emphasizing the collective journey. The addition of "Together" reflects the brand's growth into a community-building organization rather than simply a service provider.

Brand Idea: "Ride Further"

Foundational Philosophy

Ride Further remains the foundational philosophy, mindset, and way of life for Love Velo. This concept encompasses both physical and metaphorical boundaries, representing the brand's belief that cycling is an adventure that ignites passion and instigates positive change. The philosophy enables customers to explore new places, meet incredible people, and tackle various challenges, facilitating growth and self-discovery.

Target Audience Philosophy

Love Velo exists for everyone that rides a bike, maintaining an inclusive approach that welcomes cyclists across all experience levels and motivations. The brand celebrates diversity within the cycling community, acknowledging different cycling journeys from childhood milestones to professional achievements. As stated in their brand messaging: "We live for the first time a kid rides without stabilisers and for the commuters who ride to their computers. We say 'Chapeau' to the friends who ride together and we give Kudos to serious cyclists on their Strava propelled rides."

Social Media Voice

Instagram DM Strategy for USA Cyclist Outreach

When reaching out to USA cycling enthusiasts in touring groups, maintain Love Velo's authentic, community-focused voice. These messages should be genuine icebreakers that share real experiences while respecting the fact that recipients don't know us yet.

Key Principles:

  • ‚Ä¢ Be genuine and conversational, not sales-focused
  • ‚Ä¢ Share authentic experiences, not marketing copy
  • ‚Ä¢ Respect their time and interests
  • ‚Ä¢ Maintain the "community of cyclists" approach
